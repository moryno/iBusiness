SIGN UP

1.       Enter your email address and click on send verification code

2.       Immediately a verification code is sent to the email provided, enter the code for verification.

3.       Enter new password and confirm.

4.       Enter your full names and phone number

5.       Enter name of the organization and the number of employees

6.       Click on create

7.       After the account is successfully created you will be routed to your dashboard
